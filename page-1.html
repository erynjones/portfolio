Visual Identity and Branding

Brand Developpement

ZombieProof is Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and MMA Company that teaches you how to self defend for yourself. The branding of the designs is stock with the Zombieproof theme while demonstrating what the company does. To help portray what services the company provides the martial art belt is used because in Brazilian Jiu-jitsu, and other martial arts, it is an essential part of the uniform. To keep with the Zombieproof theme there’s a hand of a zombie to that is coming out of the belt, similar to how zombie hands come out of the ground bringing the whole design together. The zombie hand and belt is used throughout the whole branding of the company to create their identity.
